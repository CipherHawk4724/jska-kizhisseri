About Us

JSKA Kizhisseri Dojo is a proud member of the Japan Shotokan Karate Association, dedicated to the practice and promotion of traditional Shotokan Karate. Our dojo is focused on building strong individuals, both mentally and physically, through discipline, respect, and dedication to the martial arts.

Our experienced instructors help guide each student on their journey towards becoming a better martial artist.
